Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna is made with layers of rich meat sauce and creamy cheese filling, baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 pack lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef in a pan.
  4. Layer the ingredients in a baking dish: noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
