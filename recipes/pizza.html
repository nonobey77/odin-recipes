Pizza Recipe

Description

This classic pizza recipe features a crispy crust, savory tomato sauce, and a blend of melted cheeses. Perfect for any pizza lover, this dish can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, olives, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  7. Let cool for a few minutes, slice, and enjoy!
