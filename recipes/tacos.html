Tacos

Description

These authentic tacos are bursting with flavor, featuring seasoned meat, fresh veggies, and a hint of lime. Perfect for taco nights or a casual gathering with friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • Sour cream and salsa for serving

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and 1/4 cup of water, then simmer until the liquid is absorbed.
  3. Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos by filling each shell with the seasoned meat.
  5. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, shredded cheese, and any other desired toppings.
  6. Serve with sour cream and salsa on the side.
